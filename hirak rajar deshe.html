About The Movie

"Hirak Rajar Deshe" is Satyajit Ray's sequel to "Goopy Gyne Bagha Byne," blending fantasy with social commentary. In the kingdom of Hirak, ruled by tyrant King Hirak, oppression reigns. Through whimsical characters and settings, Ray critiques authoritarianism and champions freedom. Goopy and Bagha, unwitting heroes, challenge the regime through music and bravery, delivering a timeless message of resistance. Celebrated for its creativity and social relevance, the film remains a landmark in Indian cinema.
